Name each reference inline; never write role essays. Slates does this for you: `@mention` a subject or environment and it composes `Marcus (images 1 and 2) in the cafe (image 3)`, citing them in the exact order it sends them. Citing both of a character's sheets under the SAME name is what tells the model they are one person — a "Reference Image Instructions" block does the opposite and drags the sheet's studio lighting into your scene. Start with 2-3 focused refs.
